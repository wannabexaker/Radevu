# Booking SaaS Platform - Master Vision Document

## Project Purpose

Create a SaaS platform for appointment-based businesses.

This is NOT:
- a website builder
- a marketplace
- a social network

This IS:
- digital secretary
- booking system
- customer organization system
- lightweight CRM
- business assistant

Goal:
Reduce phone calls, paper notes, forgotten appointments and chaos.

---

## Main Concept

Each business gets:

- public mini-site
- business dashboard
- booking management
- customer management
- reminders
- notes/history
- statistics
- notifications

Example:

giannis-barber.radevu.gr

Business profile can be shared on:

- Google Maps
- Instagram
- Facebook
- QR code
- websites
- e-food
- Wolt
- messages

---

## Public Business Profile

Visible to customers:

- logo
- photos
- services
- prices
- available dates
- contact information
- social links
- booking button
- location
- working hours

Customer flow:

Open link
Select service
Select date
Select time
Enter information
Confirm
Receive reminder

Target booking time:
Less than one minute.

---

## Business Dashboard

Main tabs:

- Today
- Appointments
- Customers
- Debts
- Notifications
- Settings

Dashboard philosophy:

No complexity.
Minimal typing.
Mostly dropdowns.
One tap actions.

## Business Strategy

Initial strategy:

Personally onboard businesses.

Personally create profile.

Personally explain usage.

Use first customers to validate assumptions.

Priority:
Large adoption.

Revenue:
Secondary.

---

## Product Philosophy

Never become complicated.

Stable interface.

Buttons should stay in same place.

Avoid frequent UI changes.

The business should feel:

"I open my phone and everything is organized."
